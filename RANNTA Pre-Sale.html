How to join the Pre-Sale

  1. Use a TON wallet such as Tonhub, MyTonWallet, or any trusted self-custody TON wallet.
  2. Send TON or USDT directly to the official payment addresses below. Only on-chain transfers from your own wallet are accepted.
  3. Keep your transaction hash and make sure you control the private keys of the sending wallet.
  4. RANNTA will be distributed manually to the same wallet address after on-chain confirmation.
