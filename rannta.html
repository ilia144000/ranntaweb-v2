Ecosystem Architecture

RANNTA began with a TON-based digital asset and has expanded into a wider infrastructure ecosystem. Each component serves a distinct role:

  • RANNTA Token: The first public asset on TON, providing liquidity, verification, and holder participation.
  • RANNTA Network: Independent infrastructure for interoperability, registry-backed services, and cross-chain coordination.
  • RANNTA X-Change: Live non-custodial cross-chain exchange with multi-provider routing.
  • RANNTAverse: Service and marketplace layer for creators and ecosystem participants.
