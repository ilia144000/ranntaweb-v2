RANNTA Airdrop

Early community members and symbolic participants can claim their share of the RANNTA Airdrop, a celebration of rebirth and decentralized meaning.

Eligibility

  • Holders of early RANNTA NFTs
  • Community contributors
  • Symbolists and lore builders

Distribution

Airdrops are handled on TON for speed and low fees. Tokens will be distributed automatically to eligible addresses.

How to Claim

Connect your TON wallet during the claim window. Verify eligibility and receive RANNTA tokens instantly.
