Burn History

RANNTA is both a memorial token and a living protocol on TON. The Phoenix burn program is designed to gradually reduce supply, increase scarcity, and symbolically mark new activation phases of the network.

Major Phoenix phases:

  • Phase 0 - 1: Early test burns and symbolic activations.
  • Phase 2: Large rebalancing of Community, Team, Advisors, Liquidity and Reserve wallets.
  • Current: More than 63% of the original supply has been sent to the null wallet.

Future burns (if any) can simply be added here as text without touching the layout or theme of the site.

  • Verify that the remaining supply is roughly 47.57B RANNTA (~36.59%).
