RANNTA Whitepaper

1. Executive Definition

RANNTA is a layered blockchain ecosystem containing three distinct but related foundations:

  • The official RANNTA token issued as a Jetton on The Open Network.
  • RANNTA X-Change, the live non-custodial cross-chain exchange.
  • RANNTA Network / RANNTA X-Chain, the separate blockchain and interoperability architecture.

RouteX is the independent routing and execution-planning engine being developed as a common language between blockchain networks, assets, execution environments and cross-family settlement paths.

2. Official RANNTA Token on TON

The official RANNTA token is currently issued only as a Jetton on The Open Network. It is the first public ecosystem asset and remains an identity, marketplace, liquidity and participation asset within the wider RANNTA ecosystem.

  • Network: The Open Network (TON)
  • Token standard: TON Jetton
  • Official Jetton Master: EQBCY5Yj9G6VAQibTe6hz53j8vBNO234n0fzHUP3lUBBYbeR
  • Verification: TON Viewer
  • Boundary: the TON token is not the native network layer of RANNTA X-Chain.

A token using the RANNTA name or ticker on another blockchain is not an official RANNTA asset unless its deployment and contract address are explicitly announced through official RANNTA sources.

3. RANNTA Network / RANNTA X-Chain

RANNTA X-Chain is the independent blockchain and interoperability direction of the RANNTA ecosystem. It is separate from the TON-issued RANNTA token and must not be classified as a TON application, middleware layer or Jetton extension.

  • Chain identity: RANNTA X-Chain
  • Chain ID: 13113
  • Native currency: RANNTA Core X (RNTX)
  • Primary purpose: interoperability, verifiable coordination, routing, state handoffs and cross-chain communication
  • Public surfaces: RPC, compatibility proxy, explorer and network status infrastructure

The network architecture is being evolved through controlled deployment, validation, node coordination, diagnostic contracts, compatibility layers and staged production activation.

Public RPC and explorer availability do not automatically imply that every planned validator, consensus, staking, bridge, liquidity or network-native service is already active. Each capability is announced separately after verification.

4. RouteX Universal Routing Engine

RouteX is RANNTA's independent multi-network routing and execution-planning engine. Its objective is to provide a permanent and extensible language for networks, assets, liquidity venues, execution plans and cross-family communication.

  • Independent runtime architecture without mandatory production dependency on Rango or Squid.
  • Permanent local network and asset registries.
  • Multi-network EVM quote and execution infrastructure.
  • Real-fork verification across supported EVM networks.
  • Solana detection, quote and unsigned execution-plan support.
  • Staged adapters for TON, Sui, Bitcoin and additional blockchain families.
  • Operator and safety gates before public signing or settlement activation.

RouteX is designed so newly created networks, assets, routers and execution models can be incorporated without restructuring the entire engine.

5. RANNTA X-Change

RANNTA X-Change officially launched on 13 July 2026 as the live non-custodial cross-chain exchange of the RANNTA ecosystem.

  • Official URL: https://ranntaexchange.com/
  • Operating model: non-custodial and wallet-controlled
  • Routing surfaces: RouteX, OneRoute and OmniRoute
  • External routing providers: Squid and Rango where integrated
  • Public surfaces: swap, warp, activity, portfolio, markets, networks, security, trust and developer information

RANNTA X-Change is distinct from Rango Exchange. Rango and Squid are independent third-party routing providers and are not the owner, identity or canonical brand of RANNTA X-Change.

Users retain wallet control. RANNTA X-Change does not require custody of private keys or seed phrases.

6. Cross-Chain Interoperability and Proof Infrastructure

RANNTA interoperability development includes controlled experiments and implementations for cross-chain messages, proofs, state handoffs, execution evidence and settlement coordination.

  • LayerZero OApp and OFT interoperability paths evaluated and tested.
  • Controlled EVM diagnostic deployment completed.
  • Replay safety, deterministic evidence and operator approval remain mandatory.
  • Bridge and settlement paths are activated only after chain-specific validation.
  • Proof and execution records are separated from marketing claims and public activation status.

7. Exchange and Ecosystem Services

The wider RANNTA service layer connects the token, X-Chain, X-Change, RouteX and RANNTAverse through shared identity, verification, routing and developer infrastructure.

  • Asset and contract verification
  • Token registry and listing workflows
  • Token factory and service-fee infrastructure
  • Cross-chain explorer and activity records
  • Network and exchange status surfaces
  • Developer APIs and routing infrastructure
  • RANNTAverse identity and creator-economy services

8. Security and Release Discipline

RANNTA follows staged implementation and activation boundaries. A configured endpoint, internal prototype, diagnostic deployment or unsigned execution plan must not be described as a fully activated production capability.

  • Signing and fund-moving actions require explicit execution controls.
  • Cross-chain settlement requires chain-specific validation.
  • Node, validator and consensus behavior must be versioned and documented.
  • Rollback, evidence and audit procedures are required for production releases.
  • Public claims must match verified runtime status.

9. Signal-13 and Mythosymbolic Fractalism

Signal-13 and Mythosymbolic Fractalism form the cultural, symbolic and narrative layer of RANNTA. They provide a visual and conceptual grammar for identity, art, participation and creator expression.

The symbolic layer does not replace technical documentation, token verification, network state or exchange execution logic. It complements the engineering ecosystem while remaining clearly separated from technical claims.

10. Conclusion

RANNTA has expanded from a TON-origin token project into a layered ecosystem containing an official token, a live non-custodial exchange, an independent X-Chain architecture, a universal routing engine, interoperability research and RANNTAverse creator services.

The operating principle is explicit: completed systems are described as completed, active engineering is described as active, and unverified or unreleased capabilities remain staged until implementation and validation are complete.
