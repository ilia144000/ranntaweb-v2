▲ Introduction

RANNTA Coin (RANNTA) is a decentralized, community-powered token built on the scalable TON blockchain. It is more than just a digital asset — it carries a symbolic message rooted in the memory of the devastating Los Angeles fire.

RANNTA represents rebirth, resilience, and a return to glory — echoing the eternal call to "Make America Great Again."

Designed for speed, affordability, and security, RANNTA aims to be a tool for financial and spiritual revival in the decentralized era.

To protect the long-term value and trust of RANNTA holders, all administrative privileges have been permanently revoked from the token smart contract. This ensures that the total supply is fixed and no additional tokens can ever be minted. The contract is immutable and fully decentralized — reinforcing RANNTA's commitment to fairness, scarcity, and symbolic integrity.

▲ Cross-Chain NFT Strategy

To expand the artistic dimension of RANNTA and bring conceptualism into crypto production, we are building a multi-chain NFT footprint alongside TON. Each chain is selected for its culture, tooling, and collector base, while all works retain verifiable provenance through hashes, C2PA signatures, and steganographic marks.

  • ○ Ethereum — flagship MSF pieces and 1/1s (OpenSea, Manifold), with EIP-2981 royalties and programmable editions.
  • ○ Solana — high-velocity drops and interactive pieces (Magic Eden, Tensor) optimized for scale and low fees.
  • ○ Bitcoin (Ordinals) — time-capsule inscriptions for enduring archival presence and mythic permanence.
  • ○ Polygon — gas-efficient editions and collabs for mainstream activations and brand integrations.
  • ○ TON — home base (Getgems) where symbolic logic meets ecosystem utility and token-gated lore.

Cross-chain releases will be synchronized via a shared registry (on TON) mapping token IDs ↔ chain ↔ marketplace, ensuring collectors can verify originals across ecosystems. Where feasible, bridges or receipts may mirror ownership states for unified experiences in future dApps.
