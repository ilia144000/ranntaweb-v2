RANNTA Whitepaper

1. Executive Definition

RANNTA is a layered blockchain ecosystem containing three distinct but connected foundations:

  • The official RANNTA token issued as a Jetton on The Open Network.
  • RANNTA X-Change, the live non-custodial cross-chain exchange of the RANNTA ecosystem.
  • RANNTA Network / RANNTA X-Chain, the independent and operational blockchain and interoperability layer.

RouteX is the independent routing and execution-planning engine being developed as a common language between blockchain networks, assets, liquidity environments, execution systems and cross-family settlement paths.

Matching names or tickers displayed by a DEX, wallet index, price page, blockchain explorer or third-party data provider do not establish official RANNTA status.

RANNTA X-Chain is the independent blockchain and interoperability layer of the RANNTA ecosystem. It is separate from the TON-issued RANNTA token and must not be classified as a TON application, TON middleware layer or Jetton extension.

  • Official chain name: RANNTA X-Chain

  • Public RPC: https://rpc.rannta.com/
  • Public Explorer: https://explorer.rannta.com/
  • Multichain Explorer: https://explorer.rannta.com/activity
  • Network status: Live and operational

The RANNTA X-Chain public JSON-RPC endpoint, compatibility layer, explorer, transaction indexing, block records, receipt visibility, activity surfaces and RNTX fee settlement are active.

The RANNTA Explorer provides live access to blocks, transaction hashes, wallet transfers, receipts, fees, treasury records and indexed network activity.

The RANNTA Multichain Explorer provides network-specific activity, verification records and native evidence across RANNTA X-Chain and connected blockchain ecosystems.

RANNTA X-Chain is designed for interoperability, verifiable coordination, routing, state handoffs, proof transport, cross-chain communication and decentralized trust between isolated blockchain environments.

Additional network capabilities such as expanded validator decentralization, staking, slashing, bridge-native settlement, network-native liquidity and broader node participation are published independently as their production deployments are completed.

4. RouteX Universal Routing Engine

RouteX is RANNTA's independent multi-network routing and execution-planning engine. Its objective is to provide a permanent, extensible and provider-independent language for networks, assets, liquidity venues, execution plans and cross-family communication.

  • Independent runtime architecture without mandatory production dependency on Rango or Squid.
  • Permanent local blockchain, network and asset registries.
  • Multi-network EVM quote and execution infrastructure.
  • Real-fork verification across supported EVM networks.
  • Solana asset detection, quote generation and unsigned execution-plan infrastructure.
  • TON, Sui, Bitcoin and additional blockchain-family adapters under active staged development.
  • Extensible architecture for newly created networks, assets, routers, liquidity systems and execution models.
  • Explicit execution controls for signing, settlement and fund-moving actions.

RouteX is designed so that newly created blockchain networks and routing structures can be integrated without restructuring the entire engine.

Its long-term role is to act as a shared communication and execution language between heterogeneous blockchain families.

5. RANNTA X-Change

RANNTA X-Change officially launched on 13 July 2026 as the live non-custodial cross-chain exchange of the RANNTA ecosystem.

  • Official name: RANNTA X-Change
  • Official URL: https://ranntaexchange.com/
  • Status: Live
  • Launch date: 13 July 2026
  • Operating model: non-custodial and wallet-controlled
  • Routing surfaces: RouteX, OneRoute and OmniRoute
  • Independent integrated routing providers: Squid and Rango
  • Public service surfaces: Swap, Warp, Activity, Portfolio, Markets, Networks, Security, Trust, Developers, Guides and cross-chain information pages

RANNTA X-Change is distinct from Rango Exchange. Rango and Squid are independent third-party routing providers and are not the owner, identity or canonical brand of RANNTA X-Change.

Users retain wallet control. RANNTA X-Change does not request custody of private keys or seed phrases.

Route selection, quotes and execution may depend on the selected networks, assets, connected wallets, liquidity, routing providers and current blockchain conditions.
