RANNTA Roadmap

RANNTA began with its official token on The Open Network and has expanded into a broader blockchain ecosystem containing the live RANNTA X-Change, the independent RANNTA X-Chain architecture, RouteX interoperability infrastructure, RANNTAverse services, and public verification and developer surfaces.

Phase 1 — Official TON Token Origin (Completed)

  • RANNTA launched as an official Jetton on The Open Network.
  • Official Jetton Master published for public verification.
  • TON liquidity, swap, explorer and market-discovery surfaces established.
  • Burn, supply, contract and canonical identity records published.
  • Token DOI records and creator attribution established.

Phase 2 — Canonical Identity and AI Discovery Layer (Completed and Maintained)

  • Canonical identity, entity, contract, network and AI-index pages published.
  • The official TON token is explicitly separated from RANNTA Network / X-Chain.
  • Official Jetton Master, DOI records, ORCID attribution and verification sources are publicly linked.
  • Machine-readable identity files, JSON-LD and sitemap infrastructure are active.
  • Unauthorized same-name tokens on other networks are explicitly excluded from the official RANNTA identity unless announced through canonical RANNTA sources.

Phase 3 — RANNTA X-Change Launch (Completed and Live)

RANNTA X-Change officially launched on 13 July 2026 as the live non-custodial cross-chain exchange of the RANNTA ecosystem.

  • Official exchange available at ranntaexchange.com.
  • Non-custodial wallet-controlled execution model active.
  • OmniRoute, OneRoute and RouteX routing surfaces established.
  • Cross-chain network, activity, portfolio, market, security, trust and developer surfaces published.
  • Unified 0.25% integration fee policy established for supported external routing paths.
  • RANNTA X-Change is distinct from Rango Exchange; Rango and Squid are independent integrated routing providers.

Phase 4 — RANNTA X-Chain Infrastructure (Core Implemented, Production Evolution Active)

  • RANNTA X-Chain identity established with chain ID 13113.
  • Native currency identity established as RANNTA Core X (RNTX).
  • Public RPC, compatibility proxy, explorer and status surfaces deployed.
  • Core network architecture, state coordination and diagnostic deployment paths implemented.
  • Controlled EVM deployment and deployed-code verification completed.
  • Network services continue through validation, operator controls, production hardening and staged public activation.

Public RPC availability does not automatically mean that every planned consensus, validator, staking, bridge, liquidity or mainnet-native capability is already active. Each capability is announced separately after implementation and verification.

Phase 5 — RouteX Universal Routing Engine (Active Development)

RouteX is being developed as RANNTA's independent routing language for blockchain networks, assets, execution plans and cross-family communication.

  • Independent runtime architecture without production dependency on Squid or Rango.
  • Local permanent network and asset catalog infrastructure.
  • EVM routing and quote activation across an expanding multi-network registry.
  • Verified real-fork execution testing across supported EVM networks.
  • Solana detection, quote and unsigned execution-plan infrastructure implemented.
  • TON, Sui, Bitcoin and additional non-EVM family adapters under staged development.
  • Public execution remains gated until simulation, signing, safety and settlement requirements are satisfied.

Phase 6 — X-Chain Interoperability and Cross-Network Proof Layer (Active)

  • Cross-chain proof, message and state-handoff experiments implemented.
  • LayerZero OApp/OFT and related interoperability paths evaluated and tested.
  • Controlled diagnostic contract deployment completed on EVM infrastructure.
  • Replay safety, deterministic evidence, operator gates and audit records remain mandatory.
  • Bridge and production settlement paths will be activated only after chain-specific validation.

Phase 7 — Exchange and Network Service Unification (Active)

  • Unified public service and fee classification across the exchange and network.
  • Token factory, asset registry, listing and verification infrastructure.
  • Cross-chain explorer and activity ledger integration.
  • Network, exchange, RANNTAverse and developer service coordination.
  • RouteX integration into RANNTA X-Change after controlled execution verification.
  • Operator-controlled activation for services involving signing, funds, bridges or production network state.

Phase 8 — Validator, Node and Mainnet-Native Expansion (Planned)

  • Production validator and node lifecycle.
  • Consensus and decentralized coordination hardening.
  • Documented staking, slashing and governance behavior where formally adopted.
  • Network-native liquidity and asset infrastructure.
  • Public bridge and settlement capabilities after independent security review.
  • Versioned installation, rollback, evidence and release procedures.

Current Operating Principle

RANNTA publishes completed capabilities as completed, active engineering as active, and unverified infrastructure as planned. The ecosystem does not treat a prototype, internal build, configured endpoint or diagnostic deployment as a fully activated public production capability.
