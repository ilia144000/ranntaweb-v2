RANNTA Roadmap

Phase 4: Ascension (2026–2028)

  • RANNTA Decentralized Exchange (DEX) launch (by 2027)
  • RANNTA Blockchain (Layer 1) development (by 2028)
  • Real-world use case partnerships and payments integration
