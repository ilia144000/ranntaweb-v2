RANNTA X-Change Officially Launched on 13 July 2026

A non-custodial cross-chain exchange connecting RANNTA's routing, wallet and ecosystem infrastructure.

Published 13 July 2026 Updated 20 July 2026

Public Availability

RANNTA X-Change is publicly available at https://ranntaexchange.com/. The launch marks a live exchange surface within the broader RANNTA token, network and RANNTAverse ecosystem.

The exchange is designed as a non-custodial cross-chain interface. Users retain wallet control, and the service does not request a seed phrase or private key.

Routing Surfaces

RANNTA X-Change exposes three distinct routing surfaces for cross-chain exchange activity:

  • RouteX — RANNTA-native routing infrastructure.
  • OneRoute — Squid-powered routing surface.
  • OmniRoute — Rango-powered routing surface.

Current ecosystem coverage is supplied by the active routing integrations and may evolve. Quotes and execution depend on selected routes, wallets, networks, liquidity and provider availability.

Ecosystem Context

Activity, network, trust, security and developer information are available on the exchange. These surfaces are intended to help users inspect available routes and understand the exchange environment before interacting with wallets or networks.

RANNTA X-Change is an active non-custodial product within the broader RANNTA token, network, and RANNTAverse ecosystem.
