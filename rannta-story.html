🔥 The Story of RANNTA

RANNTA was born out of fire — a mythic symbol forged in the crucible of destruction, re-emerging with purpose and vision.

🌟 Origins

RANNTA symbolizes rebirth and resilience. Inspired by the eternal cycle of ashes and flame, it encodes meaning into blockchain permanence.

📜 Philosophy

At the heart of RANNTA is Mythosymbolic Fractalism (MSF) — a philosophy that blends myth, fractal logic, and symbolic art to create multi-layered meaning across digital and physical realms.

🌐 Journey

RANNTA evolves across ecosystems: TON as home, Ethereum for art editions, Polygon for accessibility, Solana for experimentation, and Bitcoin for permanence.
